Paternity Leave is over and above any other form of leave and Male employee(s) will be entitled to five[5] working days of paternity leave when blessed with a child or in the event of adoption of a child, subject to submission of necessary documents regarding the adoption, failing which, such leave will be treated as void. This leave should be availed within thirty[30] calendar days from the delivery/adoption of the child and can be availed as continuous five [5] days or spread across a span of thirty [30] calendar days. Like any other leave, this leaves too will require prior approval of the immediate manager and can be combined with the Earned Leave or Compensatory Off. In the event of separation, a male employee will be entitled to paternity leave only upon receiving a prior approval from the immediate manager, while serving notice period.
